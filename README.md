# Senior SWM GIS Platform

**Empowering Bengaluru's Senior Citizens with an Accessible, Interactive GIS Web Map for Solid Waste Management**

## Why This Project Matters

Rapid urbanization in India combined with increased youth migration for employment opportunities has dramatically changed family structures — shifting from traditional multi-generational households to nuclear families. As a result, many senior citizens are left isolated with limited daily support.

Seniors frequently struggle with solid waste management (SWM) due to:
- Unreliable door-to-door collection services
- Difficulty locating waste bins
- Lack of clear, accessible information

These challenges lead to heightened feelings of isolation and a loss of autonomy.

This project was created to solve these real-world problems by designing and developing a **free, senior-friendly webpage** with an interactive Geographic Information System (GIS) map. The platform integrates municipal datasets to:
- Reduce daily struggles through an intuitive and accessible interface
- Improve service reliability by minimizing missed or delayed collections
- Enable seniors to manage solid waste and related tasks independently

## Project Goals

- Reduce daily challenges and social isolation for Bengaluru’s urban seniors in solid waste management through an easy-to-use GIS webpage.
- Enhance the reliability of door-to-door SWM services by leveraging real-time visibility and predictive features to cut down on missed or delayed collections.

## Project Objectives

- Launch a **free, web-based GIS platform** that is accessible and simple for senior citizens to use, thereby reducing their daily struggles and feelings of isolation.
- Improve door-to-door service reliability by **reducing missed or delayed waste collections by at least 40%** using real-time visibility and predictive alerts.
- Enable senior citizens to manage SWM more independently — successfully completing key tasks such as finding the nearest bin or tracking a missed collection directly on the interactive web map.
- Achieve coverage of **at least 70% of high-senior-density wards** with active platform usage.

## Project Scope

The project includes the full design, development, and deployment of a dedicated website featuring an interactive GIS web map tailored to solid waste management challenges.

### Core Features
- The platform provides rich ward-level maps displaying:
- Ward-level interactive map
- Schedule viewer
- Historic Grievances
- Advanced filters 

**Additional Design Standards**
- Senior-friendly interface (large fonts, high contrast, simple navigation)
- Full WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility compliance

## Visualization


- Bin locations
- Vehicle routes
- Service coverage areas

It also overlays essential urban amenities for better context:
- Parks
- Recycling points
- SWM Offices
  

Special emphasis is placed on senior-dense wards to ensure the platform delivers maximum impact where it is needed most.
